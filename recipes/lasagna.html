Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Butter/Oil
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Fry beef in butter/oil
  3. Add tomatoes, chopped onion and garlic to the beef. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Mix pasta, beef and 2/3 of the cheese in a pan
  5. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top of everything
  6. Bake in the oven for 20min.
  7. Enjoy!
